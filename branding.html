Branding

This is a branding for a flower shop. The colours I chose to be fresh , bright and eye catching. I used bigger wordmark and smaller symbol in logo which makes it easier to read and emphasize. I chose font with serif which has a flourish attached to the end of a stroke in a letter, is classic and illustrates establishment and makes them a great choice for the flower shop. The flower symbol is viberant and communicates the level of freshness that customers can expect from Fina flowers.The line are clean and strong to communicate with customers with the newly cut flowers they get. I chose the symbol to be tulip shape for it’s beauty and a good symbolize for a growing business due to their bulb, they keep coming back and multiple. The symbol has a rounded shape to give it a unique graphic shape that is the wordmark.
